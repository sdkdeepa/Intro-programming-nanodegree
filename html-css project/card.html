Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skin makes it immune to the fish-asting anstrongone's leathal sting.

  • Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae
  • Average Length: 11 centimeter
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Habitat: Tropical coral reefs

Clownfish or anstrongonefish are fishes from the subfamily Amphiprioninae in the family Pomacentridae. Thirty species are recognized one in the genus Prstrongnas, while the rstrongaining are in the genus Amphiprion. In the wild, they all form symbiotic mutualisms with sea anstrongones.
